Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

Do’s

1) Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
2) Think before posting — ensure content is respectful and appropriate
3) Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your information
4) Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
5) Maintain a professional online presence (important for internships & jobs)

Don’ts

1) Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
2) Avoid posting content that is offensive, harmful, or illegal
3) Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
4) Avoid oversharing your location or daily routine
5) Do not trust or click on suspicious links or unknown messages
