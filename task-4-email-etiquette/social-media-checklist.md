Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

Do’s
Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
Think before posting — ensure content is respectful and appropriate
Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your information
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
Maintain a professional online presence (important for internships & jobs)

Don’ts
Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
Avoid posting content that is offensive, harmful, or illegal
Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
Avoid oversharing your location or daily routine
Do not trust or click on suspicious links or unknown messages
